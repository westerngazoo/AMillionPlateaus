# Fourier Analysis

Fourier analysis decomposes a function into sinusoidal waves. The integral
transform bridges pure mathematics, the physics of signals, and the harmony of
sound.

Reference: [The Fourier Transform (paper)](https://example.org/fourier.pdf)
and [3Blue1Brown on Fourier](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=spUNpyF58BY).
