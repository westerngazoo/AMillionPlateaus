    Browser

    You hold a wizard key (Nostr/BIP340) minted in this browser: its public half is your stable id; the secret stays local and is never displayed, synced or logged. Your career lens only orients you — it lights each faced domain's trailhead, no free rank. Click a lit plateau to sign a traversal; reach is then recomputed from your verified signed history by the GA core — earned, not gifted. Vouch for a discovered wizard to flow your earned reach to them (mutual-only vouching collapses to nothing — Sybil resistance). Connect a relay to publish/discover across hosts, or stay offline — the world plays either way. Reputation, keys and signatures never enter the CRDT; only graph state (plateaus, bridges, votes) crosses the graph-sync channel, while signed events ride a separate channel + the relay.
